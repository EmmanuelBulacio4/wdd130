White Water Rafting

All Dry Oar guides know the rivers of Argentina extensively and are recognized as the best professional staff in the country. They are experienced kayakers and rafting guides. They have taken courses in first aid given by the International Red Cross and rescue technicians in rapid water and floods, given by Special Rescue Services and World Rescue Group (I.R.I.A.). All are guides certified by the International Rafting Federation. Some of them have work experiences abroad.

History

Dry Oar was founded by Emmanuel Bulacio, a passionate about adventure sports. A pioneer in this sport in the state, he decided to take his passion further by putting rafting available to everyone. Throughout these years we were able to improve our facilities and achieved the most demanding certifications worldwide and specific insurance for adventure activities..
